Who is Fuchaweb3?

Fuchaweb3 is the leading decentralized education platform dedicated to the Hausa-speaking community. We believe that technology and finance should not have a language barrier.

Our Mission

To accelerate Web3 and Crypto adoption across Northern Nigeria and the Sahel by providing verified, high-quality information in a language our people understand best.

Our Vision

To create a future where every Hausa speaker, regardless of their location, has equal access to the opportunities within the global digital economy and blockchain ecosystem.
